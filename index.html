"COKE ADDS LIFE"

Coca-Cola is a globally renowned beverage company known for its signature carbonated soft drink. Established in 1886 by John Pemberton, Coca-Cola's iconic red and white logo has become synonymous with refreshment worldwide. The company offers a variety of beverages including Coke, Diet Coke, Coca-Cola Zero Sugar, and an array of flavored sodas. Additionally, Coca-Cola has diversified its portfolio to include water, juices, teas, and energy drinks. Over the years, the brand has maintained its position through innovative marketing campaigns, strategic partnerships, and a commitment to sustainability.
